## Capture checklist

- Record in English with clear voiceover; music alone is invalid.
- Show actual interaction, not slides standing in for unimplemented behavior.
- Use only the public-safe starter pack for import; never show private world-bible text or local paths.
- Keep the final video below three minutes; judges may stop at 3:00.
- Hide notifications, browser profile details, keys, raw logs, and `/feedback`.
- Upload early, wait for processing, and verify the public/unlisted link in a private window.
