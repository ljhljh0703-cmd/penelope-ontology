## Writing-positioning line

Use this idea in narration without making a vendor comparison claim:

> We did not ask the model to magically remember a world or discover our voice. We encoded style, character knowledge, canon, approval, and state transitions as separate harness layers.

An unbounded-versus-style-profile comparison may appear only if both runs and the human rubric have been captured. Do not say Codex or GPT-5.6 writes better than Fable, Opus, or another model.

## Capture safety

- use the hosted or clean local demo only
- hide keys, terminal history, personal paths, browser profiles, and private tasks
- do not show the full Codex conversation or `/feedback` ID
- label fixture and live traces visibly
- label participant IDs as synthetic local inputs, not remote accounts
- show actual behavior; do not substitute a slide for an unimplemented feature
