## What it is

Narrative Knowledge Harness is a Work & Productivity web tool for professional GMs, narrative production teams, writers, and game scenario or quest designers. It combines several participant intents inside a bounded fictional world and creator-owned style without treating every plausible-sounding addition as canon.

## How it works

A facilitator records participant intents separately from the fictional characters they control and selects an original style profile. A typed World Pack records entities, atomic claims, source traditions, fixed events, character knowledge, and creator rules. GPT-5.6 receives only the relevant character views and returns attributed prose, assertions, actions, and proposals through a strict schema. Deterministic validators check identity, state, time, location, knowledge, active traditions, and unsupported expansion. A derived canon/knowledge graph explains the result. Creator-approved changes alone enter the overlay and a two-step finite-state simulation; frozen cases then replay. `[EVIDENCE: vertical-slice, style-profile, multi-intent-attribution, graph-view, gpt56-integrated, state-transition, replay-regression]`

## Demo

The demo uses two fixed moments from Greek mythology rather than an encyclopedia-sized world. Three local participant intents become a restrained Ithacan scene candidate. The graph exposes character-knowledge boundaries and an original red-sail rule proposal. After creator approval, two validated steps advance `harbor_watch` from `idle` to `watching` to `signal_seen`, while living-Hector, Penelope knowledge-leak, and Helen-conflict cases retain their expected outcomes. `[EVIDENCE: demo-cases, two-step-replay]`

## Why Codex and GPT-5.6

Codex was used to translate narrative voice, canon, character knowledge, participant ownership, and world-state transitions into schemas, deterministic code, UI, tests, and evidence artifacts. Instead of accepting generic default prose as the finished product, the workflow makes creator-owned style constraints, character perspective, world truth, and approval separate harness layers. The creator retained product, original style, canon, scope, subjective writing judgment, and submission decisions. GPT-5.6 produces bounded structured candidates; deterministic code owns validation and transition. This is evidence of controllability, not a claim that one model is the best writer. `[EVIDENCE: clean-core-session, response-metadata, style-profile]`
